Verdict: Should You Sell?

Recommendation: Sell BEFORE 23 Jun 2026 if you plan to sell at all

The market is active (13 listings, quick turnover) and your car is at the sweet spot — ~5 years old, PARF still at 75%, ~5y COE remaining. Waiting past June costs you ~$2,700–$3,000 in PARF rebate and will lower buyer interest as the car enters its 5th year.

Reasons to Sell NOW (before Jun 2026)

  • PARF rebate at 75% — lose ~$2,700–$3,000 after Jun 23
  • 5-year-old car stigma avoided — buyers prefer "under 5 yrs"
  • Market is buoyant: asking prices $142k–$153k for Jun '21 equivalent
  • 23-Jun-21 M-Sport listing active at $142,800 — market already validated
  • COE ~5y 2m left enhances buyer confidence
  • High COE environment keeps 2021 resale values elevated

Reasons to Keep / Sell Later

  • Only ~$2,700–$3,000 PARF difference — not catastrophic if you love the car
  • Annual cost of ownership lower than depreciation if car is paid off
  • BMW X3 still well-regarded; holds value relatively well vs. peers
  • Selling now means finding a replacement at very high new car prices
  • If mileage is low (<60k km), your car fetches premium — no rush
